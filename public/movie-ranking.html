The Best Anime According to Brian

My top 3 anime of all time.

Sword Art Online (SAO)

A captivating tale of a beautiful VRMMORPG turned living nightmare with real life-or-death stakes.


That Time I Got Reincarnated As A Slime

An awesome tale of fun and adventure, being given a second chance to delve into a fantasy isekai world.


Bungo Stray Dogs

A gritty, supernatural seinen with deep themes around mankind and the darker states of its being represented in the social hierarchy ruled by the strongest Gifts.
